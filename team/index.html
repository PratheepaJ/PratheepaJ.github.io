Our research team addresses critical challenges in modern datasets and multi-view learning, with a strong emphasis on developing innovative methodologies motivated by real-world applications in microbiome multi-omics, spatial multi-omics, spatio-temporal sensor data, and loss reserving using multiple loss triangles.

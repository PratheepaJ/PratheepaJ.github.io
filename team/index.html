Jeganathan Lab studies multi-view learning through the lens of dependence modeling, developing latent variable and spatio-temporal methods for complex dependent data. Our work combines statistical theory, scalable computation, and machine learning to build interpretable, reproducible, and well-validated tools, with applications in spatial omics, longitudinal microbiome data, transportation sensors and GPS data, and loss reserving using multiple loss triangles.
